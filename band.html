F5 Band Members

Brendon - Singer/Guitar

Brendon fell in love with The Beatles at 9 years of age and has been singing ever since. Passionate about all things music, he fronts Foreman's 5 as singer, guitarists and musical director.
“Music is a synthesis of passion & energy; it's pure, focused love in action. This is how we express our music so that your event is filled with every bit of passion and excitement you desire. Our pledge as a band is to bring that focus every time we perform...”



Cheri - Singer

Originally from Massachusetts, Cheri's family moved to Florida where she began her professional career in music. Cheri is the type of performer that moves you, literally. Her presence and energy is quite contagious and her broad vocal range and ability to sing so many styles is inspiring. Cheri performed at MTV's Spring break headquarters, Club Levela in Panama City with her tribute band No Clout, the No Doubt and Gwen Stefani tribute band. She has had the pleasure of working with many talented artists in the music industry as well as working in commercials as a model. Cheri brings energy and passsion to the F5 lineup!



TJ - Singer/Bass

TJ brings a unique energy to F5 with his 4, 5 and 6-string Bass guitars combined with his always fun, explorative approach to music.
“F5 is a great opportunity for me because it's comprised of extremely talented musicians. We are able to push the boundaries of many genres with our diversity, giving the audience not only a great party, but an engaged and entertaining show.”



Colby - Singer/Drums

Colby Peters is a multi-instrumentalist from Orlando, FL. Primarily a drummer/songwriter, he was a founding member of both SWFL rock groups Phoenix Falling and Galia Social, and has played with numerous local cover acts. He is currently working on the debut Galia Social EP and recording with various groups in the Orlando area in addition to performing with F5. Colby also teaches private drum lessons and is available for studio sessions and live performances.



Tyson - DJ
